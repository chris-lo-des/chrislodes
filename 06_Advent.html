Advent 2018

In late 2018, I worked with a small team to develop identity and collateral for the church's four-week Advent series which leads up to the Christmas service. The goal was to capture some of that old-school Christmas charm, combine it with the true Christmas message, and land it convincingly in contemporary New York. The final identity spread spanned digital media—slides and social media assets—to print assets like handout bulletins. As a takeaway for the Christmas service, I also designed a special edition 28-page newspaper which contained our full Advent devotional, custom illustrations, and a "classified" section for various upcoming church activities.
